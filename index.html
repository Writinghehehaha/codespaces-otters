Why Otters Are The Best!

By Giselle F

Just look at those fluffy faces!

Facts

  • Did you know that mother otters will blow hot air into the fur of their young to help them stay warm? Alongside, it helps the babies stay afloat!

  • They have the thickest fur of any animal, which means that you'll definitely get some fluffy pets out of them :)

  • Otters hold each others hand to keep them from drifting away from each other! How adorable is that?
